Chem Guard

Chem Guard features a smart gas mask with real-time monitoring and remote connectivity, supported by an app for status tracking and location.

Yuhui Qi
Product Design / Usability Testing / Speculative Design
2023
/ 01 OVERVIEW

Gas masks are critical for safeguarding individuals from harmful airborne substances across contexts such as military operations, petrochemical industries, firefighting, and epidemic prevention. Yet conventional masks suffer from notable limitations: they are often bulky and heavy, uncomfortable for long-term wear, and their static filtration systems cannot adapt to rapidly changing environmental threats. As a result, protection is inconsistent, and user performance is compromised.

Chem Guard reimagines the gas mask as an intelligent protective interface. It integrates real-time monitoring of environmental gas composition, adaptive filtration, on-site image capture, and seamless remote connectivity. Supported by a companion mobile app, the system allows users to track equipment status, communicate in real time, document hazardous environments through photos, and share precise geolocation.

Beyond immediate protection, this project frames Chem Guard as a step toward a new category of wearable human–environment interfaces: devices that not only shield the body but also actively mediate between environmental intelligence and human cognition.
