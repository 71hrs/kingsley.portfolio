/ 01 OVERVIEW
During my time as a Creative Designer at McCann Worldgroup, I had the privilege of collaborating with esteemed clients such as Armani Beauty and Valentino Beauty. Working closely with these high-profile brands, I was responsible for designing their daily social media tweets and contributing to engaging campaigns. Notably, our team played a crucial role in facilitating Valentino Beauty's successful entry into the Chinese market.
